STEWRD Heritage Copilot
Open-source · Free to use

AI tools for heritage property custodians

No conservation training required. Photograph, assess, and document your heritage property in minutes.

A1
Agent 1
Condition Assessor
Upload a photo. The AI classifies the condition of the building element — Good, Fair, Poor, or Critical — and tells you what to do next.
A2
Agent 2
Condition Report Digitizer
Turn a paper condition report into a structured digital record. Fill in the form on-site or from your notes.
A3
Agent 3
Maintenance Planner
Generate a prioritised maintenance schedule from your assessment records.
